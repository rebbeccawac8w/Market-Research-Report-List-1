<p><p>The vacuum leak test system market features two primary types: ultrasonic leak detectors and helium leak detectors. Ultrasonic leak detectors identify leaks by capturing high-frequency sound waves emitted by escaping gases, making them effective for pinpointing leaks in various applications. Helium leak detectors, on the other hand, use helium as a tracer gas to detect minute leaks, notable for their sensitivity and accuracy, particularly in high-vacuum environments. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring system integrity across industries.</p></p>
