<p><strong>The Can Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The can coating additives market is divided into two main types: solventborne and waterborne. Solventborne coatings utilize organic solvents as the primary carrier for pigments and binders, offering excellent adhesion and durability but raising environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. In contrast, waterborne coatings use water as the main solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC levels. Both types are used to enhance the performance and aesthetic qualities of can coatings, catering to different regulatory standards and customer preferences.</p></p>
