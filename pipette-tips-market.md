<p><p>The pipette tips market is divided into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered tips are designed for general use, providing accurate liquid handling without barriers, suitable for many laboratory applications. In contrast, filtered pipette tips incorporate a built-in filter to prevent liquid contamination and protect the pipette from aerosols, making them ideal for sensitive tasks or working with volatile substances. Both types are essential for ensuring precision and reliability in laboratory experiments.</p></p>
