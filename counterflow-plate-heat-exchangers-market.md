<p><p>Counterflow plate heat exchangers are classified into several types based on their specific functions and applications. Ordinary counterflow plate heat exchangers facilitate efficient heat transfer between two fluids moving in opposite directions, maximizing thermal efficiency. Enthalpy counterflow plate heat exchangers, on the other hand, recover both sensible and latent heat, making them suitable for applications involving humidity control.
