Single Port tourniquets utilize one access point to control blood flow, making them simpler and lighter, suitable for basic applications. In contrast, Dual Port tourniquets feature two access points, allowing for more complex procedures and enhanced control over multiple limbs or areas simultaneously. This dual functionality often leads to improved efficiency in emergency and surgical settings, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
