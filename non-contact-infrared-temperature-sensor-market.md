<p><p>The non-contact infrared temperature sensor market is primarily divided into two main types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Thermopile Infrared Sensors. Through Hole Thermopile sensors are designed for traditional circuit board designs, offering robust performance in environments where space is less of a constraint. In contrast, SMD Thermopile sensors are compact and suited for high-density applications, facilitating automated assembly and providing enhanced thermal performance, making them ideal for modern electronics and consumer devices.</p></p>
