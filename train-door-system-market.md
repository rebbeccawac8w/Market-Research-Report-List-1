<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The train door system market is categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic systems allow doors to open and close independently, enhancing operational efficiency and passenger convenience. They often use sensors for safety and are commonly found in modern trains. Semi-automatic systems, on the other hand, require some manual control, typically operated by the train conductor. This type offers a balance between functionality and cost, making it popular in older or budget-conscious train models.</p></p>
