<p><p>The Diagnostic and Interventional Cardiology Devices market comprises various specialized tools designed to enhance cardiovascular procedures. Transcatheter angiography devices visualize vascular structures, while intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) devices assess vessel conditions using sound waves. Guide wire-based systems, like fractional flow reserve (FFR) devices, evaluate blood flow and pressure in arteries. Intracardiac echocardiography (ICE) devices provide imaging within the heart, and optical coherence tomography (OCT) devices offer high-resolution imaging. Robotic-assisted surgical devices enhance precision in cardiac interventions, improving outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
