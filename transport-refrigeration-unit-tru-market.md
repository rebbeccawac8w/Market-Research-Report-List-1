Van TRUs are typically smaller, designed for urban deliveries, ensuring quick and efficient transport of cold goods. Trailer TRUs attach to larger vehicles, enabling long-haul transportation of bulk refrigerated cargo, making them crucial for supply chain logistics.</p></p>
