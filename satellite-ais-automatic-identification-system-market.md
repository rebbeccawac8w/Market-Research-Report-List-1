<p><p>Satellite AIS (Automatic Identification System) enables the monitoring of maritime vessels through satellite technology, enhancing situational awareness in marine navigation. The market is segmented into Class A and Class B transponders. Class A transponders are mandatory for larger vessels and provide comprehensive data, including vessel identity and position. Class B transponders, used by smaller boats and recreational craft, offer limited information but are cost-effective. Both types play crucial roles in maritime safety, traffic management, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
