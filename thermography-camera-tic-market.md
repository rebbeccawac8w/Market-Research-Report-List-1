<p><p>Thermography cameras (TIC) are essential tools for detecting temperature variations, utilized in various applications such as building inspections and medical diagnostics. The market is divided into cooled and uncooled TICs. Cooled TICs employ cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and offer higher resolution, making them suitable for specialized applications like industrial monitoring. In contrast, uncooled TICs operate at ambient temperatures, are more compact, and are cost-effective, catering to a broader range of commercial and residential uses.</p></p>
