<p><p>The biobased transformer oil market primarily consists of two main types: natural ester transformer fluid and synthetic ester transformer oil. Natural ester transformer fluid is derived from bio-based sources, such as vegetable oils, offering excellent biodegradability and a lower environmental impact. Synthetic ester transformer oil, on the other hand, is manufactured through chemical processes, providing enhanced thermal stability and superior electrical properties. Both types serve as environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional petroleum-based oils, catering to the growing demand for sustainable solutions in electrical applications.</p></p>
