<p><p>The sterile medical paper packaging market includes several types designed to ensure product safety and sterility. Pure paper packaging is composed entirely of high-quality paper material, often used for wrapping and protecting sterile instruments and supplies, ensuring breathability and moisture control. Blister paper packaging, on the other hand, combines paper with a plastic component, providing a secure barrier while allowing visibility of the contents. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining hygiene and extending the shelf life of medical products.</p></p>
